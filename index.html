Rebars play a vital role in modern construction, reinforcing concrete structures to withstand tensile forces, bending, and seismic loads. By enhancing the tensile strength of concrete, they ensure structural integrity, durability, and safety. Powerscreen Service Team Triumphs Over Extreme Conditions to Complete Installation in Ladakh, India

Powerscreen®, a global leader in mobile crushing, screening, and conveying equipment, successfully installed a PT400X Jaw Crusher, 1000 Maxtrak Cone Crusher, and Chieftain 1700 Screen at Umling La in Ladakh, India, one of the world’s most challenging environments. Situated at 19,024 feet, near the China border, this Border Roads Organisation project aimed to produce road construction materials under harsh conditions like low oxygen, hilly terrain, and temperatures of -23°C. Powerscreen's advanced design allowed dismantling and reassembly for easier transport, and the service team completed installation in just 15 days. The equipment is now aiding crucial infrastructure development, improving connectivity and socio-economic conditions in the region. Learn More about this.
